What is Panem?

Panem is a country founded out of the ashes of North America. After nuclear war and natural disasters tore the Earth apart, Panem was established by the last remaining humans on North America. Eventually, it grew to be a large nation and was divided into thirteen different districts.

Map of Panem

Districts of Panem

  1. District One
    District One is in charge of creating luxury goods and decoration that decorate the Capitol and other important buildings in Panem. Its citizens have excellent craftsmanship as demonstrated through their beautiful goods.
  2. District Two
    District Two is in charge of masonry and other things related to working with stone. The rumor accusing our Peacekeepers of being trained here is false.
  3. District Three
    District Three is in charge of manufacturing technology for Panem. The citizens of District Three are remarkably good engineers and are fantastic in their production of electronics.
  4. District Four
    District Four is in charge of fishing and providing fish to Panem. Although its services are appreciated, it is a district of traitors that seek to tear Panem down. Do not feed into District Four's rebellious spirit.
  5. District Five
    District Five is in charge of providing power and electricity to all oF Panem.
  6. District Six
    District Six is in charge of making transportation vehicles for Panem.
  7. District Seven
    District Seven is in charge of providing the nation with wood and paper. Its citizens are hardworking and are also skilled with axes.
  8. District Eight
    District Eight is in charge of production of textiles. Similarly to District Four, it is full of rebels that wish to take down Panem.
  9. District Nine
    District Nine produces grain for Panem.
  10. District Ten
    District Ten raises livestock and sends meat to the Capitol.
  11. District Eleven
    District Eleven's main industry is agricultures, having land covered in fields and orchards. Most product is sent to the Capitol.
  12. District Twelve
    District Twelve mines and provides coal for Panem. The two winners of the 75th Hunger Games, Katniss Everdeen and Peeta Mellark, are from this district.
  13. District Thirteen
    Before it was annihilated, District Thirteen was in charge of mining granite. It was bombed following its participation in the First Rebellion against Panem. Contrary to the belief of some, District Thirteen does NOT remain operational and did NOT manufacture nuclear weapons.
