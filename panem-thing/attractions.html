Attractions in Panem

When visiting Panem, there are many notable attractions that you may want to visit, including some of Panem's districts.

Districts


District One

District One is one District we highly recommend visiting. Witness the excellent craftsmanship and fantastic jewelry made by citizens of District One.

District Three

District Three is another District we highly recommend visiting. The skillful engineering displayed by its citizens is a must-see on any visit to Panem.


The Hunger Games

The largest event in Panem is the annual Hunger Games. Visiting Panem during the Hunger Games is a magnificent idea as you get to watch the games as they happen in real-time.
Watch our skilled Tributes compete for survival and winning for their district in Panem's annual Hunger Games.
SPECIAL! This year's Hunger Games is a once-every-25-years event named a Quarter Quell. This time, watch past victors return to the arena as they compete to win this special event!
